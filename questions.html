Recent Questions & Answers

Q: How do I start ML without a math background?

— Rohan, BCA student

A: You don't need advanced math to start! Learn basic statistics (mean, median, variance) and linear algebra (vectors, matrices) as you go. Start with Andrew Ng's course - he explains the math intuitively. You got this!
Q: Which laptop should I buy for Data Science?

— Priya, 2nd year

A: Any laptop with 8GB RAM (16GB preferred) and i5/Ryzen 5 works. You don't need expensive laptops - Google Colab gives free GPU for training models. Save your money for courses instead!
Q: How to get research opportunities at IIT?

— Amit, 3rd year

A: Cold email professors with: 1) Why you're interested in THEIR research 2) What skills you have 3) One specific paper of theirs you read. Most professors reply if you show genuine interest. Start early!
